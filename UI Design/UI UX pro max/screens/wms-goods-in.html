WMS · Goods-In Receiver

Scan-first goods-in for warehouse operatives

The receiver sees one PO at a time, with a colour-coded status stripe on every line so the state of the shipment is obvious across the entire list at a glance.

Green — complete Full quantity received and matched. Line is considered closed and won't prompt the scanner.
Amber — partial Some units received but short. Operative can continue scanning or flag a discrepancy.
Red — not started Zero units booked in. Prominent so missing lines are never accidentally completed.
One-tap scan CTA The SCAN ITEM button is permanently pinned — accessible even with gloves or in low-light conditions.
